App Features

ContactManager is a professional contact management tool providing the following core features:

Contact Deduplication

Intelligently detect and merge duplicate contacts, supporting detection by phone number and name.

Contact Management

Easily modify, delete, and edit contact information with batch operations support.

Contact Merging

Smart merging of duplicate contacts automatically preserves the most complete information to avoid data loss.

Import & Export

Import contacts from system address book, export contacts in VCF format for backup or sharing.

Sync to System Address Book

Sync app contacts back to system address book, enabling cross-device synchronization via iCloud.

Backup & Restore

Create local backup files, restore address book data anytime, with backup history management.

Privacy & Security

Data security is our most important commitment:

  • Completely Local Operations: All contact processing, modification, merging, and deletion happen locally on your device
  • No Cloud Upload: The app does not upload your contact data to any third-party servers
  • System Sync: Cross-network transmission only supports iCloud service through iOS system framework for system address book synchronization
  • Data Control: You have complete control over your data and can delete or export it anytime
  • Transparent Permissions: The app only requests contact access permission when needed, with clear and defined purposes

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will the app collect my contact information?

A: No. The app processes contact data only locally on your device and does not upload to any servers.

Q: Will merging contacts cause data loss?

A: No. Our smart merge algorithm automatically preserves the most complete information, including all phone numbers, emails, addresses, etc.

Q: Where are backup files stored?

A: Backup files are stored in the app's documents directory and are not uploaded to the cloud (unless you manually sync via iCloud).

Q: How do I export contacts?

A: Select the "Export" feature in the app to export contacts in VCF format and share via AirDrop, email, etc.

Q: Is syncing to system address book secure?

A: Yes. Sync uses the iOS system framework and is protected by system security mechanisms, with network transmission only through iCloud services.
